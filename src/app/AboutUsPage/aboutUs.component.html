Get to know our scattered squad

Hey there,

I'm Sierra Nguyen, the founder of visitVietnam. I wanted to take a moment to share with you the story behind our website and how it all came to be.

It all started with my dad, Hung Nguyen, and his years of dedication in capturing stunning visuals of places he traveled to along our S-shape country of Vietnam, from the northern highlands to the southern beaches. Those images spoke volumes about the beauty and diversity of our homeland, and I couldn't help but feel a deep desire to share it with the world.

Living abroad in the U.S as a teen and now in Switzerland, I often found myself longing for the sights, sounds, and flavors of Vietnam. Yet, it wasn't just a longing for home that stirred within me. It was a burning desire to redefine the narrative surrounding Vietnam, to unveil its hidden treasures beyond the pages of history books, beyond the shadows of the Vietnam-American war. That's when the idea of visitVietnam took root in my mind. I wanted to create a platform that would not only showcase the extraordinary landscapes and cultural heritage of Vietnam but also provide travelers with the resources they need to plan their own unforgettable journeys.

Bringing my vision to life required a dedicated team, and that's where my brother, Harry, and my best friend, Duy, came in. Despite being scattered across different countries, we shared the collective passion for unveiling the true essence of Vietnam and the belief that we could create something truly special together.

In May 2023, we embarked on this incredible adventure. Our goal is simple: to inspire and guide travelers, allowing them to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of our culture, discover hidden gems, and forge unforgettable memories.

Join us on this journey as we celebrate the beauty of Vietnam and warmly invite you to experience it firsthand.

Welcome to Vietnam.

Sierra Nguyen

Founder of visitVietnam

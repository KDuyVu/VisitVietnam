Day 1:

  • Arrive in Da Nang and check into your accommodation.
  • Explore the city center, visit the iconic Dragon Bridge, and enjoy a stroll along the Han River.
  • Try local delicacies for dinner at one of the street food stalls.

Day 2:

  • Spend the day relaxing at My Khe Beach, known for its beautiful sandy shores and clear waters.
  • Take a dip in the sea, sunbathe, or engage in water sports activities.
  • Enjoy fresh seafood at one of the beachside restaurants.

Day 3:

  • Explore the Marble Mountains, a cluster of limestone hills with caves and pagodas.
  • Hike to the summit for panoramic views of the surrounding area.
  • Visit Non Nuoc Stone Carving Village to witness skilled artisans creating intricate sculptures.

Day 4:

  • Take a day trip to Ba Na Hills and enjoy the scenic cable car ride to the hill station.
  • Visit the Golden Bridge and explore the French Village.
  • Engage in various activities such as visiting amusement parks, gardens, and cultural performances.

Day 5:

  • Discover the vibrant Han Market and browse through a wide range of local products and souvenirs.
  • Visit the Museum of Cham Sculpture to learn about the ancient Cham civilization.
  • Explore the Han River Bridge and catch the mesmerizing evening light show.

Day 6:

  • Explore the Linh Ung Pagoda located on the Son Tra Peninsula.
  • Admire the breathtaking views of the city and coastline from the viewpoint.
  • Visit the Monkey Mountain and interact with the playful monkeys.

Day 7:

  • Visit the Cham Islands, a group of beautiful islands off the coast of Da Nang.
  • Enjoy snorkeling, swimming, and relaxing on pristine beaches.
  • Experience the local island life and savor fresh seafood.
